DENTAL IMPLANTS

At the core of our business is the design and production of dental implants, equipments and dentistry solutions.

  • We can manufacturer Dental Implants based on our drawings, and also we are able to do with your drawings.

  • We also can offer Surface Treatment, Packaging, and Sterilization.

  • All products are manufactured with high quality materials, and checked and controlled one by one.

  • We make implant styles and compatible parts similar to most major Implant Companies.

  • Our designs are created to help stabilize and create a better seal at the Inter-Attachment Connection so that long term gingival health can be maintained.

Titanium Implants, Prosthetic Components, Gold Cylinders, Drills, Taps, Ratchet Wrenches, Driver Instruments
